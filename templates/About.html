Our Story

Founded in 2020, BarryShop began as a simple solution to a complex problem: inventory management shouldn't be complicated. What started as a small project to help local retailers has grown into a comprehensive platform serving businesses across the country.

Our journey began when our founder, Barry Johnson, witnessed his family's retail business struggling with outdated inventory systems. He envisioned a solution that combined simplicity with powerful features, and BarryShop was born.
